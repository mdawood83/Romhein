About Us

About Us

Romhein Construction Inc was established in 1999 as a renovation contractor and by 2012 grew to also supply the province of Quebec with superior quality uPVC and aluminum windows and doors. By 2014, Romhein Construction Inc has established supply of fine aluminum windows and doors and became the only supplier that could dye aluminum profiles in any color from the RAL color chart.

25 Years of Personalized Experience

Experience is a deal-breaker when it comes to selecting general contractors. Romhein Construction Inc’s skilled craftsmen have years of hands-on industry experience. We are poised with immense skills and resources to deal with all phases of construction and renovation projects.


As general contractors in Montreal, we separate ourselves from the clone-style general constructors by maintaining a high standard of excellence and commitment. It is our business to make your project durable, and unique. That’s why we maintain a steadfast belief in the value of good old-fashioned hard work. We pride ourselves on completing the project on-time and on-budget. Though the job is complete, our relationship with you is not - we always stand behind our work and offer continued support long after project completion.

Mission

To deliver high quality, cost effective projects on schedule by employing and supporting motivated, reliable, and focused teams. We are committed to providing the highest level of professionalism, service response, and quality workmanship while building long-term relationships with our clients, employees, suppliers and partners.

Vision

We are a family owned company, committed to becoming the supplier and contractor of choice, pursuing excellence through professionalism, dedication and knowledge of the latest industry developments with an ongoing passion to deliver superior quality, easy to use, aesthetically appealing products and services.
